Research Interests

Continual Learning, Computer Vision, Deep Learning.

Hi again, I am working on Continual Learning. Continual Learning is a specific field of AI where we teach our models with sequential data. What I mean by 'sequential data' is this: instead of assuming we have access to lots of data at once, we assume that the data comes to us part by part. We generally name this streaming data as "task". Our goal is to keep training our model with upcoming data (yes basically finetuning! ðŸ¤­). But there is a big problem here! As humans we also have this problem when learning new things. Can you guess what that is? It is of course the forgetting issue ðŸ™ƒ. When we keep learning the new task we unfortunately forget previous things. So, in continual learning we try to solve this forgetting issue while learning to new things. By saying "we" I mean many enthusiastics in this field.
