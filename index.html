Elif Ceren Gok Yildirim

Hi, I am a PhD candidate under the supervision of Joaquin Vanschoren at the department of Mathematics and Computer Science in Eindhoven University of Technology, the Netherlands.

Research Interests

Deep Learning, Continual Learning, Computer Vision.

Enigma of Incremental Learning from a Scientific Lens. Harnessing the human capacity for learning from experiences and adapting knowledge to novel challenges, my research focuses on the learning-forgetting-transfering realm of deep learning. In particular, I aim to unravel the intricacies of how these networks behave when confronted with the sequential execution of multiple tasks in the field of computer vision. By exploring this landscape, I seek to contribute valuable insights into the underlying mechanisms that govern the continual learning process of neural networks.

Autonomy in Learning Agents. Striving to move beyond the impracticality of training a dedicated agent for each task, I specialize in designing neural networks that learn automatically and continuously. This approach rejects the constraints of task-specific training, promoting a more sustainable and adaptable learning paradigm.
